What is Hague Apostille? What is consular legalisation?

The pathway depends on the destination country. Hague destinations typically use Apostille. Non-Hague destinations often require authentication + consular steps.

Hague Apostille (Apostille)

For Hague Convention destinations, Apostille is commonly used as a simplified legalisation method (replacing many consular steps in typical cases).

Non-Hague (Authentication + Consular)

For non-Hague destinations, the typical route is authentication + consular (where applicable). Example: Malaysia follows authentication + consular legalisation route, not Apostille.

Nationwide AU handling

We can coordinate documents issued in any Australian state/territory. Requirements may vary depending on receiving authority and document type.
